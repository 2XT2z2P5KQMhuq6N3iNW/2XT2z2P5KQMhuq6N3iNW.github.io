recipe for Pound Cake ~~*
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

2 cups granulated sugar
2 cups Swan's Down Cake Flour or Martha White~
1 cup Crisco
6 eggs
1/3 cup Orange Juice
1 teaspoon vanilla
~~
heat oven temperature to 300 degrees
add all ingredients except eggs into mixing bowl
beat 2 eggs at the time adding each pair to other ingredients
coat tube or bundt cake pan with non~stick spray
pour batter into prepared pan
bake at 300 degrees for about one hour
