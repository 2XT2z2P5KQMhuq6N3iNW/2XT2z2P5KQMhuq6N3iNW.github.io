Lemon Bars ~*

Step 1:
Combine the following ingredients & press
into a 10x13 pan.......

1 cup butter
1/2 cup powdered sugar
1/2 table spoon salt
2 cups flour

Bake 20 min @ 350 degrees then pour in
lemon mix...........

Step 2:
Mix together:
4 eggs
2 cups sugar
4 tablespoons flour
5 tablespoons lemon juice

Bake 20 minutes longer @ 350 degrees, then
dust with powdered sugar and cut into bars ~*
