Jeanie Brenzerekkar's Olde Worlde
Fashion Sugar Cookies

1 & 1/2 cups sifted all purpose flour
1/2 tsp. baking powder
1/2 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp baking soda
3/4 cup sugar
1/2 cup shortening
1 egg
2 heaping tablespoons milk
1 tsp. vanilla
~~
*
set oven temperature to 375 degrees.............
sift together flour, baking powder, salt,
soda & sugar............
cut in shortening until mixture resembles
cornmeal.............
blend in egg, milk & vanilla............
roll on floured board..................
cut with cookie cutters...............
place on cookie sheet...............
Bake @ 375 degrees for 8 minutes..............
makes approximately 3 dozen
cookies............
