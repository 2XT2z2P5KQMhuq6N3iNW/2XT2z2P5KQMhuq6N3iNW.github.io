                *~ Peach Cobbler ~*

1 stick butter
2 cups sugar, divided
3/4 cup flour
2 teaspoons baking powder
dash of salt
3/4 cup milk
4 cups sliced peaches
Lemon juice to taste
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
Melt butter in 2~quart casserole
Combine 1 cup of the sugar with flour, baking powder and
salt........
Add milk to flour mixture and stir..
Pour this over melted butter in casserole, but do not stir...
Add lemon juice and remaining sugar to peaches.....
Spoon peaches over the batter, but do not stir.......
Bake at 350 degrees for 45~60 minutes......
Serve warm topped with vanilla ice cream........
