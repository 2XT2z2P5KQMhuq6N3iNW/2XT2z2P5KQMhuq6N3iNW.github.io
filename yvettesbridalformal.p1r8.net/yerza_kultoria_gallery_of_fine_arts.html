being sent out to all the World !! ~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*
if you are an Artist and+or Writer and+or Fashion Designer or if
you are an aspiring Artist and+or Writer and+or Fashion
Designer you may email images and writings of your Works
and Your Creations to the Yerza Kultoria Gallery of Fine Arts by
clicking on this Pink Link { irishowl1127@aol.com } and we
shall Publish Your Works and exhibit Your Creations here at
the Yerza Kultoria Gallery of Fine Arts for FREE !! ~*~*~*~*~*~*
the Mission of the Yerza Kultoria Gallery of Fine Arts is to help
promote Fashions, Arts and Literature around the World !! ~*
we want to give EVERYONE an opportunity to Publish their
Works and exhibit their Creations to help make the World an
ever increasingly Beautiful place in which for us all to Live !! ~*
whether you are already Published and Famous or whether you
are desirous of making your Artistic debut to the World, the
Yerza Kultoria Gallery of Fine Arts is for YOU !! ~*
there are no fees, no obligations, no fine print, no strings
attached whatsoever; all we ask is that the works you send us
have a General Audiences Rating, since Yvette's and the Yerza
Kultoria Gallery of Fine Arts are Family Friendly and Family
Oriented  !! ~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*
we shall be delighted to Publish images of your Drawings,
Paintings, Sculptures, Wood~Carvings, Pottery, Gowns,
Tuxedos, Suits and other Fashion and Garment Designs you
have created as well as your Poems, Prose, Essays, Short
Stories, Children's Books and any Fine Literature of Your
Composition which you want to share with the wonderful
World !! ~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*
if you are writing or have written a Novel you want Published,
the Yerza Kultoria Gallery of Fine Arts will Publish the Title and

Fine Arts !! ~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*
if you have a fortuitous Feng Shui that you would like to share
with the World, we shall Publish it for you right here @ the
Yerza Kultoria Gallery of Fine Arts !! ~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*

through which to gain additional, or your very first, exposure

right here at the Yerza Kultoria Gallery of Fine Arts !! ~*~*~*~*~*
we shall Publish your images of Your Fine Art~Works and Fine
Designs and Your Fine Writings along with your name and
contact information if you so desire !! ~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*

fine also !! ~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*
