Big Brothers Big Sisters of Northwest Florida is preparing for Corks ‘N Canvases,

a wine tasting and art auction event to benefit Big Brothers Big Sisters of Northwest Florida.

The event takes place on Friday, February 5, 2010, from

6:00-9:00p.m. at The Boatyard on Grand Lagoon.    



We will again auction artwork created by “Littles”, with the help of their “Bigs”, in a variety of

art mediums. We will also have a silent auction of pieces donated by local artists. In addition

to the silent auction, there will be a wine tasting, light hors d’oeuvres, and music.
