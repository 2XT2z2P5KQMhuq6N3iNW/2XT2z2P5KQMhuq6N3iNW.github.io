One Three Five (The Odd Theory)

One
Alone.
One is a time to think.
One is a time to cry.
Tired and scared,
One is the way you were born.
One is the way you will die.
 
Three
Burden.
Three is with some friends.
For a time it will do.
When it gets late
Three becomes them and you.
 
Five
Outcast.
Alone from the start.
You are on the outside looking in.
Soon forgotten,
Entangled by the Odd Theory,
Is a life that will never win.


 

Through My Eyes

A world of love
In which you do not some how belong.
The passing of days,
The darkness of nights,
A life that could be put in song.
This you can see through my eyes.

The sound of rain.
The drop of a tear.
A silent wind
Blowing through your barren heart.
The fog as it draws near.
This you can hear through my eyes.

Always cold.
Alone, so scared and sad.
A hollow man
Just trying to exist.
For him, death won't be so bad.
This you can feel through my eyes.

Rejection.
That pain will cut like a knife.
Like a knight
Without a crusade
You will meander through out this life.
This you can live through my eyes.

-Chuck in Chicago
