Silent Tears

Crying tears with sound
so none can be known
Silent tears cried in darkness,
shed alone

Shed without sound,none can be heard,
shed in darkness, so none can can see
tears just the same ,
fall silently

One after another,
silently they fall.
Tears in vain are these,
unanswered will be their silent call

Maybe if written of
these tears will tell,
what I couldnt say
when my Silent Tears fell
