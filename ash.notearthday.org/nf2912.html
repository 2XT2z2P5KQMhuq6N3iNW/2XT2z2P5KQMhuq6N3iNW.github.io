Never Again to Weep

Oh to never weep,
at long last sleep.
Never again to repast,
to sleep at last.

Through the icy chill,
never again to feel.
Buried deep,now to sleep,
never again to weep.

The worms now come,
I their feast.
Oh worms come repast,
feast upon this heart of mine.

Unworthy now, I come to shame,
none to know my name.
Buried deep, now I sleep.
