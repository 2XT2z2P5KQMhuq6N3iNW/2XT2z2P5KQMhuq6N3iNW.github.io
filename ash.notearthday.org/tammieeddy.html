Daddy Never Left Me

Mommy, Why did you have to do this to me, and leave me all alone?
Now my wrists are bleeding and my voice hardly has a tone.
All I wanted was for you to love me from the start.
Now my life is almost over and I will soon depart.
As I lie here on the Bathroom floor,
I know I can't take this pain anymore.
Mommy, Why won't you look at what I made for you?
Is this to much for you to handle? Now you know how I feel too.
I am sorry I have to leave you this way.
But, Now I must go up to Heaven with Daddy, and there is nothing more to say.

-Tammie Eddy
