If Time Never Passed

If time never passed and I could know the way,
Would I venture back to those far better days?
When I felt new and there was no pain.
But would I have to live this same life again?

Did smiles once come easy? was I fearless and bold?
Were addictions just fictions, not truths to be told?
Was love was ever more than a betrayal of trust?
Or a one off encounter to dissipate lust?

No, I can never go back to when innocence was real,
And my demons mere nightmares, not cravings to feel.
I have to look forward and try to understand,
And when I find serenity, I’ll die by my own hand.

(1999 - Inspired by an excellent poem from ~Stephie189)

-Michelle Noeud
