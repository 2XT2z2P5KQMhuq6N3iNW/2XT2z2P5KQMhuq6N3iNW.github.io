inane thoughts (1993)

will an angel come to visit?
when you've taken your last soul?
will your tragedies all betray you?
cause your past had to unfold?

when you're lonely will you miss me?
when you're lonely will you cry?
when you're lonely will you want to kill me?
when you're lonely will you want to die?

 

Scars (1994)

you constantly bring my attention
to the scars of your crucifixion

perhaps i should heal them
with the kiss of a dagger?

i know someday i'll hear you screaming
at the end of that long empty corridor

i'll sit silently by
as i watch you slowly die
