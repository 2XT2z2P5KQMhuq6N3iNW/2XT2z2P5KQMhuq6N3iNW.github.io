I Have No Fear of Death

I have no fear of Death
I welcome His embrace
I stand here eagerly awaiting
The final closing of my eyes

I have no fear of Death
For in my mind I’ve died
My heart is cold and cruel
No tears can I shed

I have no fear of Death
I only fear my life
I don’t want to face the day
I can’t bear to embrace the night

I have no fear of Death
For I have sought him out
Now I patiently wait
For the day that He finds me.

-mayel
