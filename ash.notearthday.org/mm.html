"Tears of Innocent"

A tear of sweet innocents traveling far away in the night,
A dream of fears and danger that grows closer in the night,
A dream of thunder and lightning roaring thoughout the day.,
A dream of a sweet innocent child who only wants to play,

A touch of love and happiness
A gift of love not yet known,
A child's lost innocents that was only taken away
As the stars above whisper in the silence of the story untold

A tear of pain and sorrow that always goes unheld,
A tear of a fallen heart that has not yet yelled,
A tear no one seams to hear
As the silence goes on in fear.

-Michelle Schweiss-

 

"Daddy's Touch"

A touch of daddy's eye
That makes a little girl cry,
A touch of daddy's hand
That only makes you want to stand,
A touch of daddy's voice
That does not rejoice,
A touch of innocents
That does not yet exist,

They say that this is love
So what happen to things like this,

A touch of daddy's eye
That lights up the sky
A touch of daddy's hand
That helps you stand
Touch of daddy's voice
That only rejoices,
A touch of innocents
That is always in existence.

-Michelle Schweiss-

 

"Standing Alone"

My heart broken in two
As I take the final step,
The ending that I wish not to see

Is coming closer to me,
Love that I once known
I know not if there is,

Destruction that goes untold
I could stay and live of hope

Of dreams and happiness
That might come back one day,
I wish you could see
Things from my eyes

For I wish not to hurt you so,
The pain I feel is deep,
The sweet and innocent sorrow
Of something that was so sweet,

How can I say good-bye
To one I love so,
I cherish in my heart

The happiness that is untold,
Never again shall I love
I will always stand alone
In a heart of a broken soul.

-Michelle Schweiss-

"The Fierce Wind"

"Seasons"

No one knows the reasons

Summer has the sun's radiant glow
Autumn is for fallen things
Winter is the first fallen snow,

Seasons are the turning
Of many things yet to be,

That you and I can't see.

-Michelle Schweiss-

 

"Deflowering"

Walking in a daze
Of fear and anger
You had forced upon me,
I will always remember
The touch of a hand
That no longer is near,
The scent of your body
Lingers on and on
Causing fear in every corner

As I try to go on,

I shiver at
The touch of a stranger,
Never to understand
What it was I did wrong,
Your rage, desire, control
Striping me form my cloths,
A anger that keeps me
Living in fear,
I asked you to stop
You did not hear,
I pleaded with you
To end the fear,

I screamed
For anyone to hear,

I begged you
To stop the pain
That now lives
Within me everyday
And on into the night,
You changed my life

for eternity,
As I close my eyes
you are always near
I wake screaming
Because I feel you

In my tears,
The pain that never

Goes away
Only waits for the next day,
Checking behind each corner
Wondering if you're there,

I sleep no more
A peaceful sleep,
My innocents
Is yours to keep
Gone by the touch of your hand,
as I try to wash away
The part of you
That lives
Within me everyday,
You took me freely
As your heart desired
At whatever cost
It did not matter to you

Left with only
The hope that one day
All the fears and pain
Will be washed away,
As I lay in my tears Crying over things I should of done To stop you

from tearing me in two.

-Michelle Schwiess-
