Crescent Moon

Crescent Moon soon will descend
As the night comes to Her end
I’m awake I’ve always been
In this fight I cannot win
But now I leave my fear behind
To feel the peace I hope to find
By killing my grueling agony
Smoothed by my bleeding body
I’ll go to sleep in my last bed
By tomorrow's dawn I’m dead

-Lachesis
