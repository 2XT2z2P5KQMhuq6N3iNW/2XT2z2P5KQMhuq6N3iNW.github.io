Bridges

The bridges are falling
Am I falling with them?
Down, always down
Have I already fallen?

You cannot tear the bridges down
Even though
You might see
That I am falling
Even though
I have not fallen yet

There will always be bridges
Try to tear them all apart
I am falling anyways

My personal bridge
Made of paper and steel
Stainless?
Probably not
